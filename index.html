Vehicle Forensics

Most shops treat the symptom. We find the source. Before we touch your car, we run a complete diagnostic — not a quick scan — to build a full picture of your vehicle's health.

Lino and his team call it "vehicle forensics": systematic, thorough, and honest. That's how we restore cars to top shape instead of sending you back next month for the same problem.

Built on Trust

LTS Motorworks was founded in 2010 with a simple conviction: Lake County deserved an auto shop that treated customers with honesty and delivered work that lasts.

Owner Lino built the shop from the ground up, taking on complex jobs other mechanics turned down. Whether it's a complete engine teardown, a European import requiring specialty tools, or a diesel — Lino's team has seen it and solved it.

Fifteen years in, LTS is still the first call for drivers across three counties who want it done right and not have to come back for the same thing twice.
